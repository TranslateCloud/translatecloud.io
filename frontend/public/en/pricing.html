Frequently Asked Questions

How is usage calculated?

We count each word in the source content. For example, a 500-word webpage costs €27.50 on pay-as-you-go (500 × €0.055). On subscription plans, words are deducted from your monthly quota.

What happens if I exceed my plan limit?

You'll be charged for overage at the rate specified in your plan. Professional: €0.055/word, Business: €0.045/word, Enterprise: €0.035/word. No service interruptionwe'll notify you before processing overage charges.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. You can cancel anytime from your dashboard. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period, and unused words don't roll over.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all subscription plans. Pay-as-you-go transactions are non-refundable once processing begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and SEPA direct debit for European customers. Enterprise clients can request invoice billing.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Professional, Business, and Enterprise plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Pay-as-you-go doesn't require a trialjust pay for what you use.
