Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 18, 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies (No Consent Required)

  • Authentication: Keep you logged in
  • Security: Protect against fraud and attacks
  • Cookie Consent: Remember your cookie preferences

2.2 Optional Cookies (Require Consent)

  • Analytics: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics, anonymized)
  • Performance: Monitor site performance and errors

3. Cookie Details

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
translatecloud_cookie_consent Stores your cookie preferences 1 year
auth_token Keeps you logged in Session

4. Managing Cookies

4.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

4.2 Our Cookie Banner

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser data and revisiting our site.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We use Google Analytics (with IP anonymization) to understand site usage. Google may set cookies on your device. Learn more in Google's Privacy Policy.
